Privacy Policy

Bubble Pop Blast – Buble Shooter

Effective Date: March 2026

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Bubble Pop Blast – Buble Shooter ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles user information. We built this bubble shooting puzzle game with a privacy-first approach. We believe your personal data belongs to you, and we have designed our App to respect that principle completely.

🔒 Data Collection Practices

We do not collect any personal data. Bubble Pop Blast is a fully offline puzzle game. No account creation is required, and no personal information is gathered, stored, or transmitted at any time.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or hardware specifications
  • Geolocation or GPS data
  • Browsing history, search queries, or interaction patterns
  • In-game statistics such as scores, levels completed, or play sessions

💾 Local Storage

The App may save your game progress, preferences, and settings (such as sound volume or current level) directly on your device. This data is stored locally and is never uploaded to any external server. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

🔗 Third-Party Integrations

Bubble Pop Blast does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, social media plugins, or tracking frameworks. The App operates independently without connecting to external services. If the App contains links to external websites, please note that those sites have their own privacy policies over which we have no control.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our App is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, we fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provisions for minors, and other applicable child protection regulations worldwide. Parents and guardians can feel confident allowing their children to enjoy Bubble Pop Blast.

🛡️ Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, processed, or stored by the App, there is no personal data for us to provide access to, correct, transfer, or delete. You are always in complete control of any locally stored game data on your device. Should privacy laws in your jurisdiction grant you additional rights, we are happy to assist — simply reach out using the contact details below.

🔄 Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
